# MVP Scope

The MVP focuses on reliable daily tracking:

- Create habits, tasks, recurrent tasks, and categories.
- Complete and review today's items.
- View a weekly planning surface.
- Use optional mood check-ins and reflections.
- See simple contextual stats.
- Receive rule-based suggestions.
- Archive, soft reset, and delete items.
- Switch light, dark, or system mode.

Out of scope for MVP: AI-generated suggestions, Google Calendar sync, subscriptions, native platform initialization, advanced routines/projects/systems, and complex analytics.
