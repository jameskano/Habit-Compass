# Non-Goals

- Do not implement full AI suggestions in MVP.
- Do not integrate Google Calendar in MVP.
- Do not add subscriptions or monetization logic in MVP.
- Do not initialize native Capacitor platforms yet.
- Do not build advanced recurrence before the MVP recurrence model is validated.
- Do not turn the app into a productivity dashboard with analytics overload.
- Do not require mood, reflections, values, roles, or weekly planning for normal use.
