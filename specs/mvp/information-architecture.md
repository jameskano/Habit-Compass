# MVP Information Architecture

## Primary Navigation

- Today
- Week
- Items

Mood remains future optional depth and is not part of current primary navigation.

## Global Access

- Settings in the top-right.
- Floating add button available from primary tracking surfaces.

## Settings Structure

MVP Settings uses this order:

1. Categories
2. Preferences
3. Security and sign-in, only when applicable
4. Data and privacy
5. Habit Compass Premium
6. Support and feedback
7. Account actions
8. Footer

Notifications are not shown in MVP. A future Notifications section may be inserted between
Preferences and Security and sign-in only after a Notifications spec exists.

## Items Structure

- Top-level tabs: Habits, Tasks, Recurrent Tasks.
- Category management and archive access are actions or filters within item workflows, not top-level tabs.

## Progressive Depth

Advanced fields and planning context should be hidden until users opt in or choose deeper configuration.
